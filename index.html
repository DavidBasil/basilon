Basilon Global Service

Who we are:

Basilon Global Service is a construction company founded in 2011. Located and operating in Tbilisi, Georgia, company's main areas of focus is construction, hotel management and bio-organic agriculture. Our main goal is to create high-quality products while focusing on rapid development and quality of the finished product.

What we do:

Construction: we have successfully completed 2 construction projects and currently work on the next one.

Hospitality: "Basilon Global Service" owns and operates "Basilon Hotel", a 17-room cosy hotel in the center of the city.

Bio-organic agriculture: our current agriculture project encompasses production of bio-organic pomegranate, fish and poultry.
